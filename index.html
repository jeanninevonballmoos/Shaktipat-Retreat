The Blessing

What is
Shaktipat?

Shaktipat is the blessing that awakens the divine within you. It is the sacred connection that reveals your highest potential — that which transcends the mind and the limitations of the ego.


The divine cannot be comprehended through reason alone, for it is vast, limitless, and subtle. However, through Shaktipat, the seeker is graced with the direct experience of this supreme energy moving within, generating inner strength, expanding consciousness, and a profound connection with all that exists.


Simple. Easy. Effortless. People from all over the world are coming to have this meditation experience.

The Teacher

An Enlightened
Presence

Born in Uttar Pradesh, in northern India, his encounter with the Shaktipat meditation system occurred in 1988, when he was initiated by the Siddha Master Swami Shivom Tirth. Since then, he has carried this sacred flame to all corners of the world.

"A simple and pure meditation method that inspires inner peace and joy. Anyone can practice it."

A Siddha Master and Master in Ayurveda, Swami Nardanand Ji Maharaj has awakened thousands of sadhaks (spiritual seekers) around the world. With a pure and compassionate heart, he tirelessly travels the planet, transmitting this blessing to all who wish to be freed from suffering and recognize their true divine nature.


Every action, every breath he takes is dedicated to the service of humanity. His joy lies in the spiritual blossoming of every being who crosses his path. He has actively participated in retreats in numerous countries — from Venezuela to Silicon Valley — bringing the message of inner awakening to all who wish to walk this path.

The Practice

Siddha Maha Yoga

Siddha Maha Yoga is a natural and spontaneous path of self-discovery. It begins with a sacred blessing — Shaktipat — and deepens with a daily one-hour meditation practice. At the heart of this journey is the activation of your unlimited inner potential: a direct connection with the divine energy that dwells within you.


This path is supported by immersive retreats, weekly online meetings, and a global community of seekers dedicated to expanding consciousness. Here, you don't walk alone: you are guided, supported, and inspired to live your highest truth.
